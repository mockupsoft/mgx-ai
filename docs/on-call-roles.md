# On-Call Roles

## Level 1 Engineer (On-call)

- Acknowledge alerts and start incident response
- Investigate using dashboards, logs, and traces
- Execute runbook steps and apply safe mitigations
- Escalate when thresholds are met

## Level 2 Tech Lead

- Coordinate response and assign tasks
- Authorize higher-risk actions (rollbacks, traffic shifts)
- Ensure incident communications are consistent

## Level 3 CTO

- Final decisions on customer impact and external communication
- Approves emergency actions and major rollbacks
- Ensures post-mortem is completed and action items are tracked
